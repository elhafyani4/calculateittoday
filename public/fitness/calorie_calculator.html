Why Use the Calorie Intake Calculator?

  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored calculations based on your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level to determine your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

  • Track Progress Easily: Regular use allows for monitoring changes and adjusting your plan as needed to stay on track.
